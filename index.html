What is Fateweaver?

Fateweaver is an AI visual novel generator that helps players and writers create branching narrative experiences. Choose a genre, time period, and character archetype, then explore an interactive story shaped by your decisions.

How it works

Start by selecting your story genre, era, and protagonist archetype. Fateweaver then generates a cinematic visual novel with story beats, scene art, player choices, and multiple endings.

Why players use Fateweaver

Fateweaver is built for fans of visual novels, interactive fiction, branching narratives, and AI-generated storytelling. It is designed for replayability, fast idea generation, and immersive story discovery.
